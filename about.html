About Me

Greetings visitor, and welcome to Zheryu.com, a page about me. I will be your guide. Fasten your seatbelts and enjoy the ride.

My name is Eric Zhou. If you can read Chinese, my name is also 周宇聪. I experienced childhood down south in San Diego, but recently have moved to the Bay Area to study Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at UC Berkeley (Go Bears!).

I love a solving challenging problems, which is why I am taking many advanced classes like Machine Learning and Efficient Algorithms. Before college, I would usually fill my time with year-long projects, such as the aerial dogfighting game I made in my senior year of highschool. Since then coursework and learning have supplanted my project ambitions. However, I hope to resume making cool things soon, and you can still check out the class projects that I've built on my projects page.

Outside of programming I shoot competitively with the UC Berkeley Archery Team, where I am the Ace Barebow shooter. I love experimenting with new techniques and finding more and more effective ways to shoot.

Aside from computers and bows, I enjoy surfing Reddit and playing with the neighborhood cat.

This summer I will be returning to the Ferring Research Institute, where I will help improve the existing database system.
